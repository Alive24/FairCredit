# Quick Start

Get started with FairCredit in minutes.

## 1. Connect Your Wallet

First, connect your Solana wallet to the FairCredit application:

1. Click the "Connect Wallet" button
2. Select your wallet provider (Phantom, Solflare, etc.)
3. Approve the connection

## 2. Create a Credential

1. Navigate to the "Create Credential" page
2. Fill in the credential details:
   - Course name
   - Description
   - Completion date
   - Other relevant information
3. Submit the transaction
4. Wait for confirmation

## 3. View Your Credentials

1. Go to the "Credentials" page
2. View all your created credentials
3. Share or verify credentials as needed
